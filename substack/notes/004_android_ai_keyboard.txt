Stopped copy-pasting text to ChatGPT on my phone.

Switched to an AI-native keyboard instead. The model sits right in the input field — hold a key, describe what I want, and it rewrites, translates, or expands in place.

The friction was invisible until it disappeared. Copy → switch app → paste → read → copy result → switch back → paste again. Dozens of times a day.

One less context switch. Same result.
