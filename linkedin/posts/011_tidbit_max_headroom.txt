💾 From Max Headroom to AI Avatars: The AI We Imagined vs. the AI We Have

🤖 Did you know?
Back in 1985, Max Headroom was advertised as “the first computer-generated TV presenter.” But here’s the twist — he wasn’t digital at all. It was an actor in makeup, mimicking what people imagined AI would look like.

40 years later, we actually have AI presenters —  and they definitely look better than Max ever did.

Funny how fiction becomes reality.
