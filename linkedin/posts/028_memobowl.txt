Lately I’ve been thinking a lot about how I manage knowledge.

I save articles, screenshots, links, and documents all the time…
but the truth is that most of them end up forgotten somewhere in bookmarks, folders, or note apps.

The problem isn’t collecting information.

The problem is knowing what’s actually new or worth my attention.

So I started exploring an idea for a personal knowledge assistant that could learn what I already know and surface only information that truly adds something new.

It’s still very early — mostly thinking, experimenting, and trying different approaches — but I find the concept really interesting.

I’m curious how others deal with this.

How do you manage all the information you save?
