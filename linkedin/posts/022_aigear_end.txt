After more than a thousand hours spent experimenting, building and exploring what’s possible with AI, I’ve decided to step back from my active role in AI gear.

This journey has been incredible.
We built a number of exciting prototypes, pushed the boundaries of what autonomous AI employees could look like, and met many inspiring people who shared the same curiosity and enthusiasm for the future of work.

To our early adopters – thank you.
Your support, feedback and willingness to experiment with us meant more than you know. You helped shape the vision, challenge our assumptions and bring our ideas closer to reality.

This is not a goodbye to AI gear – the project will continue to exist and explore its own direction – but it is the right moment for me to shift my focus and move forward on my own path.

I’m incredibly grateful for everything we learned along the way.
And I’m excited for what comes next.
